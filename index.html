Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI disease prediction system

Our project uses advanced AI and machine learning algorithms to analyze symptoms and predict potential diseases with high accuracy, helping users identify health issues early.

Our AI model has been trained on thousands of medical cases and achieves an accuracy rate of over 92% for common diseases. However, it's important to consult healthcare professionals for definitive diagnoses.

You can try our interactive demo above by selecting symptoms and clicking the "Analyze Symptoms" button. The demo will show you how our AI system works and provides risk assessments.

We use a combination of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch for the AI model, Flask for the backend API, and modern web technologies (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript) for the frontend.

Our model is trained on a diverse dataset of anonymized medical records, with features including symptoms, patient demographics, and diagnostic outcomes. We use transfer learning and continuous training to improve accuracy.
